Uttara Kannada

Uttara Kannada is a fifth largest district in the Indian state of Karnataka, It is bordered by the state of Goa and Belagavi district to the east, Sivamogga district and Udupi district to the south,the Laccadive sea to the west. Karwar is the district headquarters, Kumata and Sirasi are the major commercial centers in the district. The district's agroclimatic divisions include the coastal plain consisting of Karwar,Ankola,Kumata,Honnavara,Bhatkala taluks and Malenadu concsisting of Sirasi,Siddapura,Yellapur,Haliyal,Dandeli,Joida,Mundgod taluks.
